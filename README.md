#  Music Store Data Analysis using SQL

##  Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing a Music Store relational database using SQL to uncover meaningful business insights. The dataset includes information about customers, invoices, tracks, albums, artists, and music genres.

The main objective of this project is to practice SQL querying techniques and apply them to solve real-world business questions related to customer behavior, music preferences, and revenue generation.

---

##  Project Objectives
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Identify top-performing music genres and artists
- Understand revenue distribution across cities and countries
- Extract insights from relational database tables using SQL

---

##  Database Structure
The database consists of multiple interconnected tables, including:

- **Customers** – Customer details and location
- **Invoices** – Transaction records
- **Invoice Lines** – Individual items purchased
- **Artists** – Music artists
- **Albums** – Album information
- **Tracks** – Individual songs
- **Genres** – Music categories
- **Employees** – Store staff information
- **Playlists** – Music playlists

These tables are connected using primary and foreign key relationships.

---

##  Business Questions Answered

Some of the key analytical questions explored in this project include:

1. Who is the senior-most employee based on job title?
2. Which countries generate the highest number of invoices?
3. What are the top 3 highest invoice totals?
4. Which city generates the highest revenue?
5. Who is the highest spending customer?
6. Which customers listen to Rock music?
7. Which artists have produced the most Rock tracks?
8. Which songs are longer than the average track duration?
9. How much does each customer spend on artists?
10. What is the most popular music genre in each country?
11. Who is the top customer in each country?

---

##  Key Insights

- Certain countries generate significantly higher numbers of invoices.
- A small group of customers contributes a large portion of total revenue.
- Rock music appears to be one of the most popular genres among listeners.
- Specific artists dominate track production within certain genres.
